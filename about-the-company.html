Definition

Bridge Health Business Services Company is a leading and specialized provider of healthcare services, medical technology innovations, pharmaceutical and medical device development, as well as the development of healthcare facilities.Bridge Health Business Services Company is a leading and specialized provider of healthcare services, medical technology innovations, pharmaceutical and medical device development, as well as the development of healthcare facilities.

The company was founded with an ambitious vision aligned with the objectives of Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030, striving to enhance quality of life and achieve sustainable health through innovative solutions delivered locally and inspiring globally.

We work to connect the healthcare sector within an integrated ecosystem of intelligent and modern solutions, adhering to the highest standards of quality and professionalism. We are committed to continuous development and to creating solutions that meet client needs and exceed expectations—supported by a team of experts who ensure precise and efficient execution that delivers the highest levels of satisfaction.

At Bridge Health, we also build long-term partnerships based on trust, reliability, and leadership, aiming to be the first choice locally and globally, and to contribute to positioning the Kingdom as a leading destination in digital health and innovative business solutions.

This journey was not the result of professional effort alone; it was driven by a profound human story that became the turning point toward establishing the company. The founder went through a painful personal experience following the passing of his mother—may God have mercy on her—due to complications from a liver transplant. This tragedy motivated him to search for innovative solutions that could improve the quality of healthcare services and accelerate patients’ access to better care.

Inspired by this human motivation, the company was established by a group of young Saudis who combined medical expertise with a passion for innovation. A number of Saudi physicians believed in the vision from the very beginning and supported the founder with their research and medical knowledge, becoming partners in establishing Bridge Health and contributing to its journey toward excellence. Through their united efforts, the company continues to grow today, building a more advanced and higher-quality healthcare future.

Company Owners

Founder

Ahmed Mohammed Al-Sulami is not merely an entrepreneur; he is the story of a young man who believed that great dreams are born from pain, and that difficult moments may carry within them a powerful message.

He combined his passion for technology with his expertise in project management and his deep understanding of the challenges facing the healthcare sector and its connection to vital national industries. The passing of his mother—may God have mercy on her—was the spark that transformed his sorrow into a driving force, inspiring him to create solutions that offer others better chances at life. From that moment, his path began: bridging gaps and building a more efficient and compassionate digital healthcare ecosystem.

Then came the vision of His Royal Highness Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman—may God protect him—which served as a source of inspiration, affirming that the energy of youth is the nation’s true wealth and its future. Thus, Ahmed continued his journey, turning pain into achievement and inspiration into innovation, writing a story of pride that inspires the youth of the entire nation.

His partners believed deeply in his vision and his determination to create real impact. They joined him by unifying their efforts and providing scientific, technical, and financial support. This shared belief came together in the founding of Bridge Health Business Services Company and the launch of its journey toward building a better future for the healthcare sector.
